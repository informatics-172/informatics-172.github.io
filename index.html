About Us

We are a world class organization dedicated to providing meaningful services to those who are victims of prescription abuse. Studies have show an upsurge in misused prescription drugs with an astonishing 18 million individuals reported just last year. More than half (53%) of prescription opioid users got their last painkiller from a friend or relative, with 40.4 percent paying nothing for the pills. Among the leading causes of death in the United States, prescription opioid drugs reigns in the top-tier, with a staggering 40 percent of all US opioid overdose deaths. This mind blowing statistic along with many others is the reason why we want to raise awareness, in hopes to help and/or prevent addiction abuse--more specifically opioid abuse.
